About Us

At Ketnno Sacco, we take pride in providing efficient and reliable transportation and package delivery services to all parts of Kenya. With our headquarters conveniently located in Nairobi at Tearoom Stage, we have been serving our valued customers since 2015

Our Mission

  • At Ketnno Sacco, our mission is to revolutionize public transportation and package delivery in Kenya by offering top-notch services that exceed our customers' expectations. We strive to connect people and businesses across the country, making their lives easier and more connected.

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to become the leading Sacco in Kenya, setting the standard for excellence in the transport and logistics industry. We aim to build a network that covers the entire nation, providing reliable and affordable services to every corner of Kenya.
